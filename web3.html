Contest List

ZkSync Contest

Participated in the ZkSync Era audit (Oct 28 – Dec 2, 2024) on Codehawks, an EVM-compatible Layer 2 rollup using zero-knowledge proofs. We have reported 7 issues out of which only two of our reports were accepted, showcasing our expertise in smart contract security.

Infrared Contest

Participated in the Infrared contest at Cantina (Jan 15–29, 2025), submitting multiple reports. Some were marked as informative, while one was classified as low and another as medium. The protocol enables users to stake their assets effortlessly and receive IBGT, a liquid staked representation of BGT, significantly enhancing BGT's utility.

Silo Contest

Participated in the Silo contest at Cantina (Jan 14 – Feb 11, 2025), reporting many issues, with most classified as informative and low. Silo Finance is a non-custodial, permissionless lending protocol that implements isolated lending markets.
